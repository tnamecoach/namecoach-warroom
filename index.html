💰 NameCoach All-Employee Commission Structure
Every person on the team — regardless of role — is eligible to earn commission. Four tiers: Referral, Sales, Delivery/CS, and Upsell/Expansion. Deal-size accelerators, monthly & quarterly team bonuses, and targeted spiffs to drive urgency. Research-backed for a 15-person early-stage SaaS team running PLG + SLG.
15-Person Team
PLG + SLG Hybrid
Everyone Eligible
Deal-Size Accelerators
Monthly + Quarterly Bonuses
8 Spiffs
Research Foundation: Based on OpenView Partners PLG compensation benchmarks, Winning by Design SaaS comp frameworks, and Everstage spiff best practices. For early-stage SaaS teams (<$5M ARR), recommended base commission is 8–12% ACV for full-cycle sales reps, referral tiers at 3–5%, delivery/CS upsell at 5–8%. Monthly bonuses outperform annual for small teams (23% more targets hit). Spiffs of $500–$2,500 deliver a 44% performance lift when tied to a specific goal and time window (BCG research).

🌱 PLG + SLG Hybrid Rules
🌱 Product-Led Growth (PLG)
Self-serve signups: No commission until conversion to paid. Product team earns $200 PLG activation bonus per 10 signups that reach "activated" status.
PLG → Paid conversion: Person who nurtures the conversion earns full Referral rate (3%) on top of any Sales commission.
Attribution: Must be logged in SFDC within 48 hours of first contact. First-touch attribution wins if disputed.
💼 Sales-Led Growth (SLG)
Outbound / enterprise: Full Sales tier applies (8–12% ACV). No cap except Combo Tier cap of $15K.
Inbound leads (marketing-sourced): Sales earns 8% (base, no accelerator). Marketing earns $250 lead-to-close bonus.
Channel partner deals: Sales earns 6% (reduced since partner sourced). Partner earns 5% per their agreement.
Split deals: 60/40 primary/secondary unless agreed and logged in SFDC before close.
